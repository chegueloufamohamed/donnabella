Yes, we ship all over the world. Shipping costs will apply, and will be added at checkout. We run discounts and promotions all year, so stay tuned for exclusive deals.

Do I need to setup an account to place an order?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track your order and makes future purchases faster.

Yes, we offer beautiful gift wrapping for a small additional fee. You can select this option at checkout.

It depends on where you are. Orders processed in the UAE will take 2-3 business days to arrive. Overseas deliveries can take anywhere from 7-16 days.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.
